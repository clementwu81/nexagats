2017 Oncotarget Treatment Monitoring治疗监测
Thermal tomography for monitoring tumor response to neoadjuvant chemotherapy in locally advanced breast cancer 热层析成像监测局部晚期乳腺癌肿瘤对新辅助化疗的反应
Wuhan University
Summary摘要

This study investigated thermal tomography as a non-invasive tool for tracking how locally advanced breast cancer tumours respond to neoadjuvant (pre-surgical) chemotherapy. By measuring changes in the internal heat source profile of the breast at multiple treatment timepoints, the technology was able to reflect tumour metabolic activity — providing clinicians with an objective, radiation-free alternative for treatment response assessment. The findings support thermal tomography as a real-time monitoring companion throughout the full chemotherapy cycle, enabling earlier identification of non-responders without exposing patients to additional radiation. 本研究探讨了热层析成像作为无创工具，用于追踪局部晚期乳腺癌肿瘤对新辅助（术前）化疗的反应。通过在多个治疗时间点测量乳房内热源分布变化，该技术能够反映肿瘤代谢活性——为临床医生提供无辐射的客观治疗反应评估替代方案。研究结果支持将热层析成像作为整个化疗周期的实时监测工具，可在不增加辐射暴露的情况下更早识别无效病例。

2014 Asian Biomedicine Comparative Study对比研究
Comparison of mammography, ultrasonography, and far-infrared thermography in screening and early diagnosis of breast cancer 乳腺X线、超声与远红外热成像在乳腺癌筛查及早期诊断中的比较
Wuhan University Renmin Hospital
Summary摘要

Conducted across 2,036 patients, this head-to-head comparison evaluated far-infrared thermography alongside the two most widely used breast imaging modalities — mammography and ultrasonography. The study assessed each modality's sensitivity, specificity, and overall diagnostic accuracy for screening and early-stage detection. Thermography demonstrated competitive performance across the cohort, with a particular advantage in younger patients with denser breast tissue where mammography is known to underperform. As a completely radiation-free option, it offers a compelling case for use as a first-line or supplementary screening tool across diverse patient populations. 本研究纳入2036例患者，将远红外热成像与乳腺X线及超声进行直接比较，评估各模态的灵敏度、特异度及总体诊断准确率。热成像在整个队列中表现出竞争性，尤其在乳腺组织致密的年轻患者中优势显著——而这正是乳腺X线已知表现欠佳的人群。作为完全无辐射的选择，它在多元患者群体中作为一线或辅助筛查工具具有重要价值。

2015 Biomedical Optics Express Journal of Biomedical Optics Large-Scale Validation大规模验证
Q-r curve of thermal tomography and its clinical application on breast tumor diagnosis — 34,977 confirmed cases 热层析成像q-r曲线及其在乳腺肿瘤诊断中的临床应用——34977例确诊病例
Wuhan University · Biomedical Optics Express & Journal of Biomedical Optics, 2015
Summary摘要

This landmark dual-publication study — the largest in the thermal tomography evidence base — validated the Q-r curve analytical framework across 34,977 confirmed breast tumour cases. The Q-r curve is Haobo's proprietary diagnostic signature derived from the internal heat source model, mapping thermal radiation patterns to characterise benign versus malignant lesions. Published simultaneously in two journals, it established the Q-r curve as a reproducible, standardised diagnostic criterion achieving 97.1% accuracy for lesions under 2cm — outperforming both mammography and ultrasonography in this clinically critical size range. This body of work remains the cornerstone of Haobo's clinical credibility. 这项里程碑式的双重期刊研究——热层析成像证据库中规模最大的研究——在34977例确诊乳腺肿瘤病例中验证了q-r曲线分析框架。两篇论文同步发表，确立了q-r曲线作为可重复、标准化诊断标准的地位，对小于2cm的病灶准确率达97.1%——在这一临床关键尺寸范围内超越了乳腺X线和超声检查。这项研究是昊博临床公信力的基石。

2015 Genetics & Molecular Research Biopsy Correlation活检相关性
Clinical applications of internal heat source analysis for breast cancer identification 内热源分析在乳腺癌临床识别中的应用
Wuhan University
Summary摘要

This study evaluated the internal heat source analysis model across 948 patients, directly correlating thermal tomography diagnoses against biopsy-confirmed pathology results. The analysis achieved 93.2% agreement with biopsy — the gold standard in cancer diagnosis — demonstrating that thermal tomography can reliably characterise breast lesions without tissue extraction. The high concordance rate positions the technology as a credible non-invasive pre-biopsy assessment tool, capable of reducing unnecessary invasive procedures while maintaining the diagnostic confidence clinicians require. 本研究对948例患者的内热源分析模型进行评估，将热层析成像诊断与活检确认的病理结果直接对照。分析与活检（癌症诊断金标准）的符合率达93.2%，证明热层析成像能够在不取组织的情况下可靠地表征乳腺病变。高度一致性使该技术成为可信的无创活检前评估工具，可在保持临床所需诊断置信度的同时减少不必要的侵入性操作。

2014 Applied Thermal Engineering Methodology方法学
A novel method of thermal tomography tumor diagnosis and its clinical practice — bio-heat transfer model with q-r curve 热层析成像肿瘤诊断新方法及其临床实践——生物传热模型与q-r曲线
Shi, Han, Liang, Wang, Li · Wuhan University
Summary摘要

This paper introduced the bio-heat transfer model underpinning Haobo's thermal tomography system, formalising the mathematical and physical principles that govern how internal heat sources (tumours) produce measurable surface temperature distributions. The authors derived and validated the Q-r curve methodology — Haobo's core diagnostic algorithm — against mammography and B-ultrasound across 4 detailed clinical cases. Published in a leading engineering journal, this work bridges thermal physics and clinical oncology, establishing the theoretical foundation on which all subsequent large-scale validation studies were built. 本文介绍了支撑昊博热层析成像系统的生物传热模型，正式确立了内热源（肿瘤）产生可测表面温度分布的数学物理原理。作者推导并验证了q-r曲线方法——昊博核心诊断算法——通过4例详细临床病例与钼靶及B超对比。该成果发表于顶尖工程期刊，架起了热物理学与临床肿瘤学之间的桥梁，为后续所有大规模验证研究奠定了理论基础。

2008 IEEE ICNC Early Detection早期检测
Value-exploration of clinical breast diagnosis using thermal tomography — sensitivity, specificity, and cold-stimulation dynamic temperature analysis 热层析成像临床乳腺诊断价值探索——灵敏度、特异度及冷刺激动态温度分析
Zhang, Li, Sun, Wan, Yao, Zhang · Wuhan University & Hubei Cancer Hospital
Summary摘要

One of the earliest peer-reviewed clinical validations of the technology, this study enrolled 355 patients across Wuhan University and Hubei Cancer Hospital to evaluate thermal tomography in a real-world breast clinic setting. Results demonstrated 90.1% sensitivity and 84.8% specificity. Notably, this paper introduced the cold-stimulation dynamic temperature analysis technique — a method that applies a controlled cold stimulus to enhance the contrast of tumour-related heat signatures, making it particularly effective for detecting very early-stage lesions that are otherwise difficult to distinguish from surrounding healthy tissue. This innovation remains a differentiating feature of Haobo's diagnostic approach. 这是该技术最早的同行评审临床验证之一，研究纳入武汉大学和湖北省肿瘤医院355例患者，在真实乳腺门诊环境中评估热层析成像。结果显示灵敏度90.1%，特异度84.8%。值得注意的是，本文首次引入冷刺激动态温度分析技术——通过施加受控冷刺激增强肿瘤相关热信号对比度，尤其适用于检测极早期病变，这是昊博诊断方法的核心差异化特征。
